Gujarat's Saree Heritage

Gujarat is renowned for its rich textile heritage, particularly famous for Patola sarees - the world's only double ikat sarees. The state's sarees are characterized by vibrant colors, intricate patterns, and traditional weaving techniques passed down through generations.

From the tie-dye magic of Bandhani to the mirror work of Kutch, each saree tells a story of Gujarat's cultural richness and artistic excellence.
